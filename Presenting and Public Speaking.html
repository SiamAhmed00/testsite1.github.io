Presenting and Public Speaking

( 0 REVIEWS ) 3students
HOME CURRICULAM ACTIVITY

Understand how to communicate in public and get people to listen to you.

For many individuals, the anxiety of presenting to a group in a non-native language can be very challenging. This course builds on your knowledge of English language communication and helps you to become comfortable communicating with multiple people in any format, whether that’s an audience of two people in a meeting room or two million via live broadcast.

This course will help you to understand and practice a few basic disciplines and techniques that will ensure you deliver any talk in a confident, concise and engaging manner.

Our Presenting and Public Speaking course will help you understand which communication style works best for you, and how to make it work in every communication situation. You will practice using various presentation formats and will learn how to use them all effectively.

This course will also help you to:

  • Understand how to get people to listen to you
  • Understand how to develop an effective talk or presentation that engages and stimulates an audience
  • Understand how to use visual aids properly
  • Understand how to answer questions credibly
