Corporate Responsibility



Our privileged position as an educational organisation gives us both a responsibility and an excellent opportunity to raise awareness and positively influence corporate social responsibility behaviour in the English language sector.

Corporate Social Responsibility is about being committed as an organisation to making a positive impact on all our stakeholders – our staff, students, representatives, suppliers, the local and wider community.

As a predominantly “virtual” business, we are already doing our best to ensure we minimise our physical impact.

Environment

As part of our commitment to minimise any negative impact on the environment, we focus on several important aspects of our day-to-day operations.

Utilities

The cleanest and cheapest energy is the energy we don’t
Air-conditioning and PCs are switched off at the mains at the end of each day. The majority of our lighting uses the most efficient LED lights.

Recycling

Separating waste, it’s a small effort that makes a big difference.
We encourage our staff and students to cut down on and recycle study materials. We use recycled ink toners and also recycle them.

Paper

Paperless is less paper!
When we print and photocopy, we do so double-sided if possible.




ABOUT APPLIED ENGLISH

Applied English is a leading provider of premium English language courses to individuals and organisations throughout Asia. We cater for adults who aspire to improve their lives and careers by improving their English fluency, whether that’s at University, in the workplace or in another country.
