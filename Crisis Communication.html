Learn how to communicate effectively during a crisis

This is a highly practical and interactive course for participants who want to learn and practice the disciplines required for engaging with the media. It is delivered by experienced former journalists and news presenters.

Using our live online platform, you will gain firsthand experience of the many formats that media interviews can take, from print through to broadcast media. You will receive real-world advice on how to handle the media, with an appreciation of the opportunities that exist for using it to your advantage.
